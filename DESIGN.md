# PetSentinel Design System

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
"Tactile Editorial" - transforming the Pet Super-App experience into a high-end concierge service.
- **Organic Asymmetry & Tonal Depth**.
- **No pure whites/greys**: "Living Earth" spectrum.
- **No 1px solid borders**: Use background color shifts.
- **Surface Hierarchy**:
  - Base: `#fff4f3` (surface)
  - Secondary: `#ffedeb` (surface-container-low)
  - Interactive: `#ffffff` (surface-container-lowest)
  - Top-Level: `#ffd2ce` (surface-container-highest)

## 2. Typography
- **Display/Headlines**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Brand Voice)
- **Body/Labels**: Be Vietnam Pro (Information Voice)
